ABOUT THE CLUB

WELCOME TO THE HOME OF CHORNOMORETS FC

The birth of the Odesa club, which has always been one of the strongest in Ukraine, was legalized on March 26, 1936, when the All-Union Committee of Physical Culture issued a resolution to create demonstration teams in a number of USSR cities to participate in the first national championship.
